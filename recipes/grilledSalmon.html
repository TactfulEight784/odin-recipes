Grilled Salmon

This is a beatiful grilled salmon with smoked butter sauce. Salmon is a very delicate fish that can easliy be messed up but with the right techniques and skill is a very delicious fish to eat.

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 3 sprigs fresh dill
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 pound salmon filet
  • 1 plank food-safe wood, duch as untreated cedar or alder wood
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 2 sprigs fresh dill, or more to taste
